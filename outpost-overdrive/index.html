Privacy Policy of Outpost Overdrive

This Application stores gameplay data locally on the User's device and uses Unity advertising services to display rewarded ads.

Latest update: 16 May 2026

Personal Data processed for the following purposes and using the following services

Advertising

Unity Ads and Unity LevelPlay
Personal Data: advertising identifiers; device or other identifiers; approximate location; Usage Data, including ad interactions; app diagnostics; and other data described by Unity for advertising, analytics, app functionality, fraud prevention, security and compliance.

Local gameplay storage

Game progress and settings
Data: saved game progress, selected units or player choices, language and display settings, in-game currency balances, reward counters and similar gameplay state. This data is stored locally on the User's device and is not intentionally sent to the Owner.

Android permissions

Network and advertising-related permissions
Permissions: Internet access, network state, Google Advertising ID and Android ad services permissions. These are used to provide and measure ads and to support security and fraud prevention.
Notification-related permissions
The current Android build may include notification-related permissions from Unity components. The reviewed version is not designed to send location-based push notifications.

Platform services

Google Play and Android platform services
Data may be processed by Google Play or Android services for app distribution, installation, updates, security, compatibility and advertising identifier functionality. The Owner does not process Google Play payment data for this Application.

Services not used in the reviewed build

No Google Analytics, AdMob or in-app purchases
The reviewed Android build does not include Google Analytics, AdMob or Google Play in-app purchase processing by the Owner.

Device data not accessed

No camera, photo library, storage or precise location access
The reviewed Android build does not request Camera, Photo Library, external Storage or precise Location permissions.

Information on opting out of interest-based advertising

In addition to any opt-out feature provided by any of the services listed in this document, Users may follow the instructions provided by YourOnlineChoices (EU), the Network Advertising Initiative (US) and the Digital Advertising Alliance (US), DAAC (Canada), DDAI (Japan) or other similar initiatives. Such initiatives allow Users to select their tracking preferences for most of the advertising tools. The Owner thus recommends that Users make use of these resources in addition to the information provided in this document.

The Digital Advertising Alliance offers an application called AppChoices that helps Users to control interest-based advertising on mobile apps.

Users may also opt out of certain advertising features through applicable device settings, such as the device advertising settings for mobile phones or ads settings in general.

Further information about the processing of Personal Data

Unity Ads privacy controls

Unity Ads may provide privacy controls during the ad experience. Users can also limit personalized advertising through their device settings where supported by the operating system.

Local saved data

Gameplay progress and settings are saved locally on the User's device. Users can remove this local data by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the Application, depending on the operating system settings.
