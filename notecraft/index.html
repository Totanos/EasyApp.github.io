Current policy

Privacy Policy of NoteCraft

This Application collects some Personal Data from its Users.

Latest update: 16 May 2026

Personal Data processed for the following purposes and using the following services

Advertising

Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads
Personal Data: Trackers; unique device identifiers for advertising, including the Google advertising ID where available; Usage Data; device information; diagnostic information.
Ad display configuration
Personal Data: Test device identifiers and app usage events related to ad display may be processed to load and display advertisements.

Notes and local app content

NoteCraft note data
Personal Data: Data entered by the User, such as note titles, note text, checklist items, tags, favorites, archive status, reminders, drawings, formatting data and endless-note content. This data is stored locally on the User's device.
App settings
Personal Data: Preferences such as selected filters, card transparency, language, background image references, review prompt state and similar local configuration data.

Media, camera and text recognition

Camera and media picker
Personal Data: Photos, videos and image files selected or captured by the User may be attached to notes, copied into local app storage and displayed inside the Application.
Google ML Kit Text Recognition
Personal Data: Images selected by the User may be processed to extract text. Extracted text may be saved as a note if the User confirms this action.

Reminders and notifications

Local reminder notifications
Personal Data: Reminder times, repeat settings, note identifiers, reminder text and optional media references may be used locally to schedule and display reminder notifications on the User's device.

App lock and device security

PIN and biometric unlock
Personal Data: A hashed PIN and app-lock settings may be stored locally. Biometric authentication is handled by the Android system; the Owner does not receive or store biometric data.

Backup, export and platform services

Google Play Store
Personal Data: Usage Data and information processed by Google when Users download, install, update or review this Application through Google Play.
Backup, import, export and sharing
Personal Data: Notes, settings, attachments and generated files such as JSON backups or PDF exports may be created, imported, exported or shared when the User chooses the corresponding function. Local app data may also be included in Android backup or device transfer features, depending on the User's device and Google account settings.

Information on opting out of interest-based advertising

In addition to any opt-out feature provided by any of the services listed in this document, Users may follow the instructions provided by YourOnlineChoices (EU), the Network Advertising Initiative (US) and the Digital Advertising Alliance (US), DAAC (Canada), DDAI (Japan) or other similar initiatives. Such initiatives allow Users to select their tracking preferences for most of the advertising tools. The Owner thus recommends that Users make use of these resources in addition to the information provided in this document.

The Digital Advertising Alliance offers an application called AppChoices that helps Users to control interest-based advertising on mobile apps.

Users may also opt out of certain advertising features through applicable device settings, such as the device advertising settings for mobile phones or ads settings in general.

Further information about the processing of Personal Data

Local notifications

This Application may display local reminder notifications that the User configures inside the Application. Users may disable notifications through their device settings, but reminders may then no longer appear.

No account registration, payments or precise device location

This Application does not require an account with the Owner, does not process in-app payments and does not collect precise device location. Reminder notifications are based on times selected by the User, not on geographic location.
